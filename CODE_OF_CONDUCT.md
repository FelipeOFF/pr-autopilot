# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique code, not people.
- Assume good faith.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, open a [private security advisory](https://github.com/FelipeOFF/pr-autopilot/security/advisories/new) or contact the maintainers via GitHub. Reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, and PRs that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
